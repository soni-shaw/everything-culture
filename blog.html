    • Toxic Manager
    The worst part about working under a toxic manager is that it can destroy your confidence.
  • Your mistakes will be nitpicked.
  • You will never be appreciated for all that you do.
  • You will not be given credit for your work.
  • You can be thrown under the bus anytime.
  • And you will never feel the belongingness which other people are feeling to their teams.
    • The only solution for this is to change your team or leave.
    Tolerating toxicity for prolonged periods will impact your mental health as well.

Community Guidelines
Please follow the community guidelines below -
1. We encourage people to write honest reviews of their teams but we expect the language to remain respetful.
2. All the reviews are examined by our team of moderators and will be published only after that.
3. We reserve the right to not publish any review that we deem unfit for the community.
4. A contributor can request to edit or remove the review that has been posted on the website by writing to us.
